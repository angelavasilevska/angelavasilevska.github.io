PROJECTS

  • Exchange Projects

    Participating in exchange projects has been one of the most enriching experiences of my life. It was not just about the educational benefits but also the sheer fun of it. I had a fantastic time traveling to new places, immersing myself in diverse cultures, and making friends from all over the world. Each project was an adventure, filled with exciting activities, collaborative projects, and vibrant cultural exchanges. I learned so much about different ways of life, broadened my horizons, and developed skills that are invaluable both personally and professionally. The memories of laughter, shared meals, and unique experiences are ones I will cherish forever.

  • Acting projects

    Participating in acting projects, whether in theatre or oratory events, is my dearest passion. The thrill of stepping onto a stage, embodying different characters, and bringing stories to life is unparalleled. I have had the immense joy of working with talented actors and directors, each project a unique blend of creativity and collaboration. The friendships built during rehearsals, the challenge of perfecting a role, and the exhilaration of engaging an audience are experiences I hold dear. Every performance, every role, every moment on stage is a testament to my love for the craft and the joy it brings to my life.
